Position : Software Engineer

Vacancy : 5 to 7

Published on: 01 April 2022

Application Deadline: 22 April 2022

Job Context :

Shanto-Mariam University of Creative Technology, the first design based private university in the region with a diverse and motivated workforce and an equal opportunity employer is seeking highly qualified, progressive minded, dynamic and experienced full time employee of various levels in the different offices.

Job Responsibilities :

  • Design & Coding applications.
  • Develop, test and analyze programming applications.
  • Analyze and rectify software errors and present precise status reports on time.
  • Build and develop applications using computer languages such as HTML, JAVA, ABAP, Visual Basic and C++.
  • Troubleshooting system errors.
  • Managing database systems.
  • Formulate efficient database retrieval systems.
  • Provide model diagrams and prototypes of programming system.
  • Update, maintain and write new computer software or algorithms.
  • Train end users to operate new or modified programs.
  • Create, manage, and maintain coding databases.

Employment Status :

Full-time

Educational Requirements :

  • B.Sc/M.Sc in computer science or related field.

Experience Requirements :

  • 0 to 2 year(s).
  • C, C++ development experience (Preferable).

Additional Requirements :

  • Interested in embedded development.
  • Good communication for teamwork.
  • Preferable of experience in all types of product development.
  • Preferable of able to follow and act in a team environment.

Working Hours :

  • Monday to Thursday 8:00 – 17:00 (8 hours) / Break time: 1 hour.
  • Friday 8:00 - 17:30 (8 hours) / Break time: 1.5 hour.

Job Location :

Dhaka

Compensation & Other Benefits :

  • Performance bonus. (* Since after one year joining)
  • Festival Bonus(2 times Yearly). (* Since after six month joining)
  • Provident funds.
  • Weekly 2 holidays(Saturday and Sunday).
  • Salary Review: Yearly.

Salary :

  • Probation period(3 months) 20,000Tk.
  • After probation period 25,000Tk. ～
